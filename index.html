About Me

Project consultant for a two hundred million plus project in Burbank, CA Retail housing project in the process of being built for 2015 located on Olive avenue Lima street. Duties: Conduct research of properties space consumption, context relations, positive and negative aspects of how the projects is situated. Research conclusions where to provide proposals to enhance project to its most efficient and economical outcomes. Proposals where for redesigning, inner courtyard and south wall to maximize space use.
